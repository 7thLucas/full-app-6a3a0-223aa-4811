## Product Overview

**Name:** Hello World

**What it is:** A minimal single-section landing page — a clean "hello world" starter page that loads fast and looks sharp. Usable as a launch teaser or coming-soon placeholder.

**Users:** Founders, developers, and makers who need a fast, polished placeholder page before their full product launches.

**Tone:** Modern, minimal, confident. No clutter. Quiet sophistication over loud marketing.

**Strategic principles:**
- One section, centered, focused.
- Fast load, no bloat.
- Sharp visual polish despite minimalism.

**Anti-references:** Avoid busy multi-section marketing pages, heavy graphics, popups, or distracting animations.
