## Design Guidelines

**Layout:** Single full-viewport section. Content vertically and horizontally centered. Generous whitespace.

**Colors:**
- Background: near-black (#0A0A0A) or crisp white (#FFFFFF) — high-contrast minimal.
- Primary text: high contrast against background.
- Subtle accent: a single restrained accent color for one small element.

**Typography:**
- Large, bold display headline ("Hello World").
- Clean sans-serif (system font stack or Inter).
- Tight, intentional hierarchy: one headline, one supporting subtext line.

**Elevation:** Flat. No heavy shadows. Subtle, tasteful only if needed.

**Components:**
- Centered headline.
- Single supporting subtext line.
- Optional minimal accent dot or divider.

**Motion:** Minimal — at most a gentle fade-in on load. No distracting animations.

**Feel:** Quiet, sharp, modern. Apple-like restraint.
